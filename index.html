Everything You Need,
One Intelligent App

From planning to booking to navigating — NaviNooks handles every detail of your trip so you can focus on making memories.

Smart Itinerary Generation

Tell us your interests and dates. Our AI creates a perfectly optimized day-by-day plan in under a second, respecting opening hours and peak times.

Flights & Hotels

Search and compare flights across all major airlines. Find the perfect hotel near your attractions with real-time pricing and availability.

Smart Navigation

Automatically calculates walking and driving routes between every stop. Real-time traffic data ensures you're always on the fastest path.

Dining & Restaurants

Smart meal scheduling finds top-rated restaurants near your route. Get personalized recommendations based on cuisine preferences and budget.

Tickets & Passes

Compare CityPASS, Go City, and individual tickets. We calculate your exact savings and recommend the best package for your itinerary.

Weather-Aware Planning

Real-time weather integration adjusts recommendations automatically. Rainy day? We'll suggest indoor alternatives that keep your trip on track.
